Listening Practice

Here you can find activities to practise your listening skills.

  1. Listening will help you to improve your understanding of the language and your pronunciation.
  2. The self-study lessons in this section are written and organised by English level based on the Common European Framework of Reference for languages (CEFR).
  3. There are recordings of different situations and interactive exercises that practise the listening skills you need to do well in your studies, to get ahead at work and to communicate in English in your free time.
  4. The speakers you will hear are of different nationalities and the recordings are designed to show how English is being used in the world today.
  5. Take our free online English test to find out which level to choose.
  6. Select your level, from A1 English level (elementary) to C1 English level (advanced), and improve your listening skills at your own speed, whenever it's convenient for you.
